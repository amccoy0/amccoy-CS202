TACA Events

As a longtime advocate for families navigating the world of special needs planning, I’m honored to serve on the Board of Directors for TACA (The Autism Community in Action). I also frequently speak at TACA events, where I have the privilege of connecting directly with parents and caregivers, offering guidance on legal and financial tools that can safeguard their children’s futures. It’s incredibly meaningful work, and I’m proud to contribute to a community so deeply committed to empowering families.
